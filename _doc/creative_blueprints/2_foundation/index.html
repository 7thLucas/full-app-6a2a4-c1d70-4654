THE FOUNDATION

The Problem

Deals slip silently. A warm lead goes cold because a follow-up was forgotten — and there's no single place that shows what's stalling.

Leads live in your head, a spreadsheet, and scattered notes. The cost isn't mess — it's lost revenue you never see.

The Solution

A deliberately simple CRM: a clean contact directory, a running interaction timeline, and a pipeline board where nothing stalls unseen.

No bloat, no enterprise setup. Logging a touchpoint takes seconds — so it actually happens.
