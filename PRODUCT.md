# MyCRM — Product

## What it is
MyCRM is a lightweight, **single-user** personal CRM for one solo operator running their own sales and relationships. It does three things well: store **contacts**, keep a running **log of interactions** with each one, and show the **deal pipeline** at a glance. Deliberately simple — no bloat, no enterprise configuration, no team seats, no collaboration.

## Who it's for
A single solo operator: a freelancer, consultant, agency owner, or solo founder. Someone who currently tracks leads in their head, a spreadsheet, or scattered notes and loses follow-ups as a result. Strictly single-user.

## The problem it solves
Deals slip silently. A warm lead goes cold because a follow-up was forgotten, and there is no single place that shows what is stalling. Recovering even one deal a month pays for the system many times over.

## Core capabilities (MVP)
**Day-one hero: the pipeline board** — the one thing the product must nail first. Contacts and the interaction timeline exist to feed it.

1. **Contacts** — a clean directory of people and companies with the essentials: name, company, email, phone, notes.
2. **Interaction timeline** — log calls, emails, meetings, and notes per contact. Fast to add (seconds, not minutes), with the full history of a relationship one click away.
3. **Pipeline board** — deals as cards that move across stages (Lead → Contacted → Proposal → Won/Lost) via drag-and-drop. Cards link to contacts and interactions so nothing stalls unseen.

## Strategic principles
- **Stay simple.** Every feature must earn its place against "is this still a simple CRM?" Resist enterprise creep.
- **Surface what's stalling.** Make a forgotten follow-up impossible to miss.
- **Fast to log.** Capturing an interaction must take seconds, or it won't happen.

## Tone & brand
Calm, clean, confident. Productivity-tool aesthetic — uncluttered, fast, trustworthy.

## Out of scope (for now)
Marketing automation, email sending/sequencing, invoicing, and complex reporting. Deliberate non-goals for the MVP.
